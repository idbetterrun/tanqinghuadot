notiee

an ios notes app that lives in your calendar

coming soon to App Store · from may 2026

an ios app that turns photos of whiteboards, slides, or meeting notes into structured notes — automatically.

snap a photo and the ai reads it (ocr + latex), then organizes everything into a title, summary, key points, and todos. it also knows your schedule: it checks your calendar and auto-links each note to whatever class or meeting you're in right now. there's a built-in ai assistant called spark that can actually do things — search your notes, manage todos, check your calendar. your current event even shows up live in the dynamic island with a countdown.

works offline (syncs when you're back online), keeps everything on your phone. built in swift + swiftui.
